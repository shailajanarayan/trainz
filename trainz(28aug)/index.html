Why TrainzNBrainz ?

Trainz N Brainz Mentoring Solutions Pvt. Ltd. is a company which started its journey in 2013, in New Delhi as a one man gang training or coaching solution for IT and related services. Founded by Anoop Narayanan, a trainer, and an enthusiast regarding new technologies for training and educating candidates. Over a span of 3 months it graduated into a team for imparting training.

Anoop took a break and polished his communication and management skills. He changed his base from New Delhi to Kerala and shifted his gear to suit the requirements of a Soft Skill Trainer to cater to the growing demand and the need of improving communication skills of the school students. He has got experience of more than a decade as a trainer, and in these eleven years he was associated with different companies and training institutes. Eventually to find soul gratification and job satisfaction, he started his own training business. Due to his honest and straight forward approach, he got his first break through in June 2015 and started providing services to a nearby school. He also took the help of a junior teacher and paid her monthly wages, who used to help him in his daily activities in the school.

The company Trainz N Brainz Mentoring Solutions got incorporated as Private Limited on 11th September 2015 with Anoop Narayanan and Shailesh Narayanan as the directors.
